OVERVIEW

Denda and Biwil are two startups owned by Engie Factory , a startup accelerator owned by the multi-national energy Corporation, Engie. Denda is the biggest e-commerce of sustainable products in Chile. Biwil is the biggest sustainability blog in Chile.

Executed a consulting service with a team of four for these two start-ups, where we applied ethnographic research to better understand the users of both web-pages. We worked directly with both startup's CEO's, Felipe Parrague (Denda) and Darcy Vergara (Biwil). Our work consisted of three stages: an exploratory investigation, the recollection and analysis of the previous phase, and finally converging.

For the first phase we conducted multiple interviews with users and experts in the field both structured and semi-structured, had participating and non-participating observation, used free-listing and open listings, used card-sorting and took field notes, and finally conducted an urban intervention.

For the second phase we coded every previous interaction and analysed it, with the help of a netnographic investigation, we built two profiles, a web profile and a purchasing profile.

For the third phase we created personas and their respective experience maps.

Presented each startup at the end with 3 opportunities which they could develop for the future in base of our investigation and personas.

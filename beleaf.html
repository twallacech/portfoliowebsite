OVERVIEW

App focused into bringing back donations to your every day life by making them simple, fun and effortless. Transforming everyday purchases into donations, every purchase turns into leafs (in-app tokens) that one can spend on any organization of your liking.

This app was developed for a university course called Investigation, Innovation and Entrepreneurship dictated by David Law, Program Director from UC Berkley. In this instance we reached the finals. Later on our team applied and won The Bridge UC, a competition that flies part of the team to Silicon Valley to take Method of Entrepreneurship Bootcamp (BmoE) at UC Berkley.

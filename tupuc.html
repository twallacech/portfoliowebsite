Overview

Platform for students at my university PUC (Pontificia Universidad Catolica) where they could generate all possible schedules automatically through an in-house algorithm. Students can also rate courses and teachers, so when making the decision as to which courses to take they can make a better, more informed choice.
Built the whole website and algorithm in just 10 days, with 5 colleagues.
